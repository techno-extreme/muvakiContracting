Types of Construction

  1. Commercial remodel/renovation is a fantastic alternative for families and property owners that have outgrown their home or desire an upgrade but don’t want to move. Muvaki Contracting believe that the best general contractor is one who believes in your vision and will dedicate a team and work hard to make your dream a reality.

  2. Our ground up construction service takes you from the design plans, to commissioning and handing over the keys to the owner. Muvaki is keen on developing a stellar reputation within the commercial construction industry with design professionals and architects, local inspectors, real estate agents and banking representatives. Not only from our finished product do we draw pride but that too is coupled with our ability to cultivate successful working relationships. Our project management and site operations teams have the experience to manage entire construction processes for any type of project.

    Muvaki provides you a single point of contact to adequately assemble, diligently manage and successfully implement your development plans. Each project is handled with extraordinary perspective and integrity, while offering the best value for new construction and meeting time frame, budget and quality expectations.

  3. Additions/extensions to satisfy maybe just the need for a few extra hundred square metres on to your building, or much, much more are also part of Muvaki’s repertoire of construction types. Whatever size room addition you are planning, you can be confident that we will deliver a high-quality finished product at an affordable price.

  4. Tenant improvements/modifications are also offered by Muvaki Contracting for both the private and public sectors, with as much or as little assistance as a client may want. If you need everything from design through to opening your doors to the first customer at business kickoff, or minor improvements, we can help.

    To commercial property owners, Muvaki can offer you tenant improvements regardless of your line of business. We emphasize quality and personal service, making sure your job is completed to your satisfaction, by indulging you every step of the way.
  5. Civil Engineering construction for readily designed and detailed projects for construction are part and parcel of the services that Muvaki Contracting offer. We have a technical astute operations team comprising of engineers, surveyors and artisans who effectively articulate detail and execute the construction to a high degree of accuracy, good workmanship and quality satisfaction of a client of any kind.
  6. Civil Engineering design and problem solving is a category in which Muvaki is well-versed. This includes the initiation of design processes to solve particular problems, tabling solutions arrived at by thorough analysis, investigation and design processes, utilizing in-house personnel or services outsourced to the suitable partner identified from the reliable pool of construction services partners boasted by our organization. The scopes include but are not limited to stormwater drainage problems, initial ground conditions analyses for proposed construction sites, deteriorating structural conditions of existing structures engineering designs for proposed construction works.
